- Pave 2.0 -- dcorn068.github.io/Pave-D3

	Pave is an interactive job explorer app using JavaScript's D3 library. 
	It aims to help high school students make informed decisions on their education and career pathways using a data-driven model of the Canadian job market.

	Client: Ministry Of Advanced Education And Skills Development Ontario

	Status: currently undergoing user testing with high school students and iterating the second prototype.
